Take a look at our Farms

We make provisions for table size eggs of different sizes and also we produce broilers chickens all year round to meet demands of our esteemed customers. The poultry birds are raised in an ultra modern and serene environment where all measures are taken to produce a wholesome eggs and broiler chickens.


We sell all kinds of fishes including the Catfish and tilapia. We have plenty of fishes to go round our customers and they are produced all year round. We also sell fingerlings of the aforementioned fishes to farmers.


We provide live snails and processed snails as well for sale 24/7. The snails are reared in a sterile environment with a well balanced feeding ranging from vegetables, fruits to concentrates. Our prices are affordable and competitive. We also offer consultation services on snail farming.


We produce all sort of vegetables, cucumbers, turmeric, pineapple, plantain, banana, yam pepper, tomatoes, pawpaw, maize etc All our products in Kelechik Integrated Farms are wholesome and organically produced.


We raise wholesome and healthy pigs for sale all year round. The pigs are raised in a clean and conducive environment where the medications, feeding and general management are top notch. The staffs are up to date with recent happenings in the world of pig production so you are rest assured that you are getting not just pork , but pig that was raised to meet all the requirements you want.

Executive Summary

Kelechik integrated farms was founded to meet the needs of people far and wide with production of wholesome farm produce from broiler birds, turkeys both processed and live, table eggs, fresh and smoked catfish,tilapia, processed and live snails, pork and live pigs, crops of all kinds, turmeric, yam, cucumbers and also we provide cold room service to meet the demand of our esteemed customers.

Mission

To train farmers and empower them so as to increase the supply of foods in the market.

Vision

To become the leading and most valued company in the animal production industry in Nigeria by raising and processing market healthy breed of farm animals and crops at competitive prices both in local and international markets.
